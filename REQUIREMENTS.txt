Private Paste System Requirements (as far as known)

Preferably a virtual environment for flask/modules (virtualenv -p /usr/bin/python27 venv)

OS:
python 2.7

Flask
Flask-SQLAlchemy
Flask-WTForms
gevent (requires libevent-devel on centos)
socketio
Flask-Login


To use:

. venv/bin/activate
python privatepaste.py
